# AI Studio - Style & Aspect Ratio Guide

## Style Cards - Visual Effects Explained

Each style card applies a distinct visual treatment to your generated images. Hover over any style in the UI to see tooltips.

### 1. **Product Photo**
**Best For:** SaaS dashboards, software interfaces, tech product marketing
**Visual Effect:** Clean, professional product shots with clear UI elements and high clarity. Perfect for showcasing dashboards, apps, or tech interfaces with crisp details.
**Use Case:** LinkedIn ads for SaaS products, product screenshots, UI mockups

### 2. **Corporate**
**Best For:** Corporate communications, annual reports, professional services
**Visual Effect:** Formal business imagery that conveys trust, stability, and professionalism. Polished, conservative aesthetic with corporate aesthetics.
**Use Case:** Company branding, executive headshots, professional environments, enterprise software ads

### 3. **Minimal**
**Best For:** Modern startups, tech companies, minimalist brands
**Visual Effect:** Clean, simple compositions with lots of negative space. Emphasizes essential elements while removing visual clutter.
**Use Case:** Startup landing pages, modern app marketing, minimalist product ads, zen/calm brand messaging

### 4. **Modern**
**Best For:** Gen-Z brands, forward-thinking companies, trendy products
**Visual Effect:** Contemporary, trendy visual style with bold colors and dynamic compositions. Energetic and visually engaging.
**Use Case:** Social media campaigns, youth-oriented products, fashion/lifestyle brands, trendy startup marketing

### 5. **Luxury**
**Best For:** Premium brands, high-ticket services, upscale marketing
**Visual Effect:** High-end, elegant aesthetics with premium feel. Rich colors, sophisticated layouts, and premium presentation.
**Use Case:** Luxury products, high-end services, premium SaaS tiers, exclusive membership campaigns

### 6. **Dark**
**Best For:** Gaming, fintech, SaaS dark mode UIs, modern tech brands
**Visual Effect:** Sophisticated dark-themed visuals. Uses dark backgrounds with accent colors, sleek and modern appearance.
**Use Case:** Developer tools, fintech platforms, dark mode app screenshots, gaming products, tech company branding

## Aspect Ratios - Format & Use Cases

Each aspect ratio optimizes your images for specific platforms and use cases. Hover over any ratio in the UI to see tooltips.

### 1:1 (Square)
- **Dimensions:** Equal width and height
- **Best For:** 
  - Instagram feed posts
  - LinkedIn profile updates
  - Facebook organic posts
  - Product thumbnails
  - Profile pictures
- **Platform Optimization:** Social media feeds, carousels, product grids
- **Typical Size:** 1080×1080px on Instagram

### 4:5 (Vertical Portrait)
- **Dimensions:** Slightly taller than wide (4 units wide × 5 units tall)
- **Best For:**
  - Mobile ads (Instagram ads, Facebook ads)
  - Instagram Stories (slightly wider variant)
  - Mobile app marketing
  - Portrait orientation ads
- **Platform Optimization:** Mobile-focused advertising, Stories-adjacent formats
- **Typical Size:** 1080×1350px on Instagram

### 9:16 (Full Mobile Story)
- **Dimensions:** Full vertical mobile screen ratio
- **Best For:**
  - TikTok videos
  - Instagram Reels
  - YouTube Shorts
  - Vertical video content
  - Mobile-first designs
- **Platform Optimization:** Vertical video platforms, full-screen mobile content
- **Typical Size:** 1080×1920px (mobile screen ratio)

### 16:9 (Wide Landscape)
- **Dimensions:** Wider than tall (industry standard widescreen)
- **Best For:**
  - Desktop web banners
  - YouTube thumbnails
  - Presentation slides
  - Web banner ads
  - Monitor/desktop displays
- **Platform Optimization:** Desktop content, widescreen displays, presentations
- **Typical Size:** 1920×1080px on YouTube, web headers typically 1200×675px

### 2:3 (Pinterest)
- **Dimensions:** Tall vertical format (2 units wide × 3 units tall)
- **Best For:**
  - Pinterest pins
  - Tall vertical designs
  - Long-form vertical content
  - Blog post featured images
- **Platform Optimization:** Pinterest (the platform strongly favors this ratio)
- **Typical Size:** 1000×1500px on Pinterest

## Quick Selection Guide

**Choosing Your Style:**
- **Tech/SaaS Products** → Product Photo or Modern
- **Enterprise/B2B** → Corporate or Product Photo
- **Startup/Trendy** → Modern or Minimal
- **Premium/Luxury** → Luxury
- **Dark Mode/Tech** → Dark
- **Simplicity** → Minimal

**Choosing Your Aspect Ratio:**
- **Social Media (Instagram/LinkedIn)** → 1:1 (square)
- **Mobile Ads** → 4:5 (portrait)
- **Vertical Video (TikTok/Reels)** → 9:16 (full mobile)
- **Website/Desktop** → 16:9 (landscape)
- **Pinterest** → 2:3 (tall portrait)

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Match Style to Brand:** Choose a style that aligns with your brand's personality
2. **Platform-First Approach:** Select the aspect ratio based on where you'll use the image
3. **Batch Generation:** Create multiple images (2 or 4) to compare styles and find your favorite
4. **Reference Images:** Upload a logo, screenshot, or brand image for better style alignment
5. **Premium Quality:** Toggle to Premium for higher-quality outputs when maximum quality matters
6. **Prompt + Style:** The prompt describes content, the style describes how it looks
